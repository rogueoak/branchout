Say it in one word. Meet Lone Leaf.
Our newest game is a cooperative, single-clue word game, and it plays beautifully on the phone that's already in your hand. You win together, or not at all.
Each round, one player is the Seeker. Everyone else sees a secret word and writes a single one-word clue, a "leaf." The catch: clue too obviously and it wilts.
How it plays
1 Everyone but the Seeker sees the secret word. You each write one clue, a single word.
2 Duplicate or too-obvious clues wilt. They're quietly removed before the Seeker ever sees them.
3 The Seeker reads the surviving clues and takes a guess. Nail it and the whole table wins.
